## General SEO Patterns

### Keyword Research Process
1. Identify seed keywords
2. Use Google Autocomplete for long-tail variations
3. Check Search Console for existing queries
4. Analyze competitor rankings
5. Prioritize by search volume + competition

### Content Optimization
- Title: Include primary keyword near beginning
- Meta description: Compelling + keyword
- H1: Clear, keyword-focused
- Body: Natural keyword usage, LSI keywords
- Images: Descriptive alt text

### Local SEO Checklist
- NAP consistency (Name, Address, Phone)
- Google Business Profile complete
- Local citations built
- Location pages for each service area
- Customer reviews encouraged
