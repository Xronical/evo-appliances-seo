🧊 Keep Your Fridge Running Smoothly!

Your refrigerator works 24/7—show it some love with these 5 essential maintenance tips that can save you hundreds on repairs:

✅ Clean condenser coils every 6 months
✅ Check door seals for leaks
✅ Set proper temperatures (37-40°F fridge, 0°F freezer)
✅ Clean the drip pan and drain hole
✅ Keep interior & exterior clean
